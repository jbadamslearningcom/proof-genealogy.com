Marriage

Edward Mathews married Mary Knox
in December 1861 in Ontario, Canada.

After Mary died, Edward married the widow Maria Sammons Jones
on October 31, 1866 in Iroquois County, Illinois.

After Maria died, he married Sabra Mae Cegal
on May 8, 1880, in Kankakee County, Illinois.

Notes

In 1870, Edward and Maria Mathews are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one child living with them, Henry C. Jones, age 13, Maria's son from her previous marriage.

After Maria's death, Edward married Sabra Mae Cegal.

In 1880, Edward and Sabra Mathews are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one child living with them, Maxwell, age 6, from Edward's previous marriage.

In the time between 1880 and 1900 Edward and Sabra moved to
Minnesota and then South Dakota; they had four children together.

In 1900, Edward and Sabra Mathews are living in McClellan Township, Jefferson County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Edward, Ethel, and Elmer.
This biography appeared in the Iroquois County history that was printed in 1880.
