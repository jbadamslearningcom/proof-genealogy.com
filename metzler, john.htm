The Benjamin family moved from Kingston, Ontario, Canada to Iroquois
County, Illinois in the spring of 1857, and John Metzler came with them.

John died in the fall of 1857, and he was buried in the Beaver Creek Cemetery.
 

John Metzler shares a gravestone with three of his
grandchildren:  Ebenezer, Sarah, and Catharine Benjamin.
