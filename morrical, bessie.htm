Notes

Bessie Morrical married Ernest Stetson in the early 1890s.  They lived in Illinois until around 1895, when they moved to Iowa.  In about 1903 they moved to North Dakota.

In 1910, Ernest and Bessie Stetson lived in Oliver County,
 North Dakota.  They had six children living with them.

In 1920, Ernest and Bessie Stetson lived in Oliver County,
North Dakota.  They had three children living with them.

In 1930, Ernest and Bessie Stetson lived in Oliver County, North Dakota.  Also
 living with them were son Ralph and his wife Edith, and two grandchildren, Ruth
and Harold.  Bessie's brother George Morrical was also with them at the time.

Some time later, Ernest and Bessie moved to Oregon.  Bessie died in Clackamas
County, Oregon in 1946.  Ernest died in Marion County, Oregon, on May 26, 1959.
