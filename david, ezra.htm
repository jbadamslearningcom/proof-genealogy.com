Marriage

Ezra David married Prudence Foster on November 22, 1854
in Camden Township, Lennox and Addington, Ontario.  Prudence died in 1858.

  After Prudence died, Ezra married Hannah Maria Rose
on March 12, 1860 in Kingston, Ontario.  Hannah died in 1870.

After Hannah died, Ezra married Eliza Ann Baker Alter on November 9, 1871
in Douglas Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.

Notes

In 1860, Ezra and Anna David are living in Wygandt Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have their son Charles living with them.  Ezra's younger brother, Charles David, is also living with them.

In 1870, Ezra David is living in Onarga Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  He has his son Charles living with him.  (Adalaide was sent to live with Elias Rose, Hannah's brother.)

In 1880, Ezra and Eliza David are living in Douglas Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one son, Arthur, living with them.  They also have Eliza's two sons, William and Sirus.  Included in the household is Ezra's oldest son, Charles, and Charles's wife Jane.

In 1900, Eliza David is living in Onarga Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  Her son Arthur is living with her.

In 1900, Ezra David is living in Florence Precinct, Lane County, Oregon.  His son Charles and family are living in the same precinct.

Eliza David filed for divorce in 1904, claiming that Ezra abandoned the marriage in 1880.

In 1910, Ezra David is living in Florence Precinct, Lane County, Oregon.  His son Charles and family are living with him.
