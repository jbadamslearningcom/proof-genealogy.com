Notes
In 1850, William Morrical is living with his parents, Washington and Mary Morrical, in Newcastle Township, Fulton County, Indiana.  He has two younger brothers living with him.

In 1860, William is living with his remarried mother, Mary Malone, in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  His younger sister, Mary Ellen, age 2, is living with them.

 In 1870, William and Serepta Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have three children living with them.

 In 1880, William and Serepta Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have four children living with them.

 In 1910, Serepta Morrical is living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  She has one son, Henry, age 22, living with her.
