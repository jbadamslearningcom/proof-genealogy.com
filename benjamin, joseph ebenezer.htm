Notes
In 1861, Joseph was living in Ernestown Township in Ontario with his older brother, William Henry Benjamin.  He was 24 years old.

In 1870, Joseph and Samantha Benjamin are living in Belmont Township, Jackson County, Minnesota.  They have one child, Ida Jane, living with them.  Living nearby are Joseph's parents and several Lockwood families.

In 1900, Samantha Benjamin is living in Hubbell, Thayer County, Nebraksa.  She has three children living with her:  Ida, William, and John.

In 1910, Samantha Benjamin is living in Hubbell, Thayer County, Nebraksa.  She has her daughter Ida living with her.

In 1920, Samantha Benjamin is living in Hubbell, Thayer County, Nebraksa.  She has her daughter Ida living with her, and her 28-year-old grandson Rolland Edwarde.

In 1930, Ida Benjamin is living in Hubbell, Thayer County, Nebraska.  Living with her is her 82-year-old mother, Samantha.
