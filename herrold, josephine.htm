Notes

In 1900, Willis and Josephine Boughan are living in Douglas Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them:  Katie, Bessie, May, and Johnnie.

In 1910, Willis and Josephine Boughan are living in Kankakee Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them:  Bessie, May, John, and Henry.

In 1920, Willis and Josephine Boughan are living in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them:  Bessie Besinden, John, and Henry.  They also have three grandchildren living with them, Frances, Elizabeth, and Josephine Besinden.

William Willis Boughan died on April 4, 1926, in Cook County, Illinois.

In 1930, Josephine Boughan is living with her daughter Anna Bissende in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois.
