Notes

In 1880, Wesley and Sarah Price are living in Anderson Township, Mendocino County, California.  They have three children living with them:  Viola, Charles, and William.

In 1900, Wesley and Sarah Price are living in Aroma Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have five children living with them:  Mabel, George, Belle, Laura, and Walter.

In 1910, Wesley and Sarah Price are living in Santa Rosa Township, Sonoma County, California.  They have four children living with them:  George, Belle, Ethel, and Walter.

In 1920, Wesley and Sarah Price are living in Santa Rosa Township, Sonoma County, California.  They have one child living with them:  Walter, age 36.

Sarah died in 1928, Wesley died on April 9, 1929.
