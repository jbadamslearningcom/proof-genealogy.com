Notes

In 1860, 16-year-old George was living with parents
Stephen and Catherine in Iroquois County, Illinois.

George served in the 150th Illinois Infantry during the Civil War.

In 1869, George moved to Minnesota.

In 1870, 27-year-old George was living in Belmont Township, Jackson County,
Minnesota.   Although he was living alone, his census entry appears next to the
households of his uncle Elijah Benjamin and his cousin Joseph.

In 1871, George married Sarah Eliza Lockwood in Jackson County, Minnesota.  Their
first child, born in about 1873, was named Theodore.  According to the census, their
second child, Cora, was born in Illinois in 1875.

In 1880, George was living in Iowa with his wife and two children.
