Marriage

Mary Jane Benjamin married Randall W. Platt on May 4, 1858 in Kankakee County, Illinois.  They had six children together; Randall died on February 17, 1881.

She then married John Hedge on April 15, 1881 in Middleport Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They had one child together before John died.

Mary Jane's third and last marriage was to John H. Evett; they married on April 7, 1894 in Watseka, Illinois.
