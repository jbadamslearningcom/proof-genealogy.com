Notes

Sylvester's wife Elizabeth was born in Pennsylvania.

Sylvester and Elizabeth were married in Ohio in the 1830s.

Their first daughter, Mary Margaret Wadley, was born in Ohio about 1837.

They moved to Indiana; Sylvia Wadley was born in Indiana in about 1839.

In the 1840s, Sylvester and Elizabeth moved their family to a farm in Waldron, Illinois (present day Aroma Park).  Sylvester worked as a cooper (barrel maker).  This area was considered part of Iroquois County, until 1854, when it became part of the newly formed Kankakee County.

After Elizabeth died, Sylvester moved in with his brother Case Wadley near Aroma Park.
