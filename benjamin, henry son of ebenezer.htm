Marriage

Henry Benjamin married Eliza Jane Powley
on January 6, 1835 in Kingston, Ontario.  Eliza died in 1871.

Henry's second marriage was to Phidelia Moore
on July 23, 1872 in Odessa, Ontario.  She died in 1886.

Henry's third marriage was to Elizabeth "Betsy" Smith Hartman
on October 24, 1886 in Ernestown Township, Ontario.
