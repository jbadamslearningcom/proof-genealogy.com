Marriage

Rebecca Powley married Zachariah David
on May 3, 1814 at Bay of Quinte, Ontario, Canada.

They were married by Reverend Robert McDowell, a well-known
Protestant missionary of the reformed Dutch church on the Bay of Quinte.

Notes
 

According to genealogist Wilman Davis, "Neither Zachariah nor Rebecca were educated -- there being no schools available.  Yet they could speak both English and Dutch. ... Rebecca learned some French through some employment with a French family in Kingston, Ontario."
