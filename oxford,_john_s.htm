Notes

In 1850, John and Mary Oxford are living in Highland Township, Vermilion County, Indiana.  They have three children living with them, Mary, Lydia, and Martha.  They also have John Gilman, John Maxwelt, and Rachel Oxford living with them.

In 1860, John and Minerva Oxford are living in Douglas Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Mary, Martha, and Sarah.  They also have Daniel Nick living with them.

In 1870, John and Minerva Oxford are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Sarah, John, and George.
