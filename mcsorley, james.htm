Notes

In 1850, James and Fanny McSorley are living in District 21, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one child living with them, James.

In 1860, James and Fanny McSorley are living in Beaver Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have four children child living with them; James, Polly Ann, Abigail, and John T.

In 1870, James and Fanny McSorley are living in Beaver Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have three children child living with them; Polly A., Ada M., and Thomas.

In 1880, Fanny McSorley is living in Beaver Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  She is living with her son James and James's two sons, James and Roy.

These blurbs were printed in the Iroquois County Times.
