Notes
Guy appears on the 1900 census as "Guy Stratton."  He was living with his mother in the household of Lewis and Abigail Morrical in Kankakee County, Illinois.

Eventually, Guy's name was changed to Guy Morrical and he was raised by Lewis and Abigail.
