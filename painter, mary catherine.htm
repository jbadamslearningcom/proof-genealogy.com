Marriage

Mary Catherine Painter married Henry Shanks on May 30, 1861 in Randolph County,
Indiana.  At the time of the 1880 census, Mary and Henry were still married, but living
at separate addresses -- Mary was living with the children, while Henry was living next
door as a boarder in the McNees household.  They later divorced.

Mary Painter Shanks married widower Edward G. Adams
on July 22, 1883, in Randolph County, Indiana.
