Marriage

Estella Goodfellow married William Wesley Ely
on June 24, 1877 in Iroquois County, Illinois.
They divorced on February 18, 1878 (recorded in Watseka, Illinois).

Estella's second marriage was to Robert Findley Addis
on November 18, 1889 in Weld County, Colorado.
