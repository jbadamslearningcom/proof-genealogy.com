Notes
In 1850, Jesse Morrical is living with his parents, Washington and Mary Morrical, in Newcastle Township, Fulton County, Indiana.  He has two brothers living with him.

In 1860, Jesse was living with another family; the family was broken up after Washington's death.  It is not known where Jesse went to live.

 In 1870, Jesse and Elizabeth Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have one child living with them.

 In 1880, Jesse and Elizabeth Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have three children living with them.

Elizabeth Morrical died in May 1894.  After her death, Jesse and family moved to Marion in Grant County, Indiana.

Jesse Morrical was still alive and living in Marion, Indiana, in 1920 when his brother Finley died.
Both of these obituaries were printed on May 24, 1894.
