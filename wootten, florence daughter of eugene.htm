Notes

In 1910, Eugene and Effie Wootten lived in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.
They had their 7-year-old daughter Florence living with them.

Florence's funeral notice appeared in the Morning Oregonian on January 18, 1916, on page 14, column 4.  It reads: "WOOTTEN -- The funeral services of Florence E. Wootten, daughter of Mr. and Mrs E.E. Wootten, of 450 Park street will be held tomorrow, (Wednesday), Jan 19, at 10 o'clock A.M. at the residence establishment of J.P. Findley & Son, Montgomery & 5th.  Friends invited.   Internment private."
