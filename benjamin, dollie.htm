Notes

Dollie appears on the 1880 census as "Rachell."

Below:  Dollie's death was reported in the Iroquois County Times.

Three young Benjamin sisters died within a four month period.
  Annie, the youngest at age 6, died first, in June 1889.  Then Dollie,
 age 11, died in August.  Bessie, age 9, died in September 1889.

 The three sisters share the same gravestone.
