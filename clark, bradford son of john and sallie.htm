Notes

In 1850, Bradford and Rosannah Clark are living in District 21, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them:  John, William, Ellen, and Sanford.

In 1860, Bradford and Rosannah Clark are living in Wygandt Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have nine children living with them:  John, William, Helen, Albert, Lucinda, Noah, Catherine, Joseph, and Bradford.

In 1870, Bradford and Rosannah Clark are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have two unmarried children living with them:  Joseph and Bradford.  They also have their daughter and son-in-law living with them:  George and Lucinda Brower.

Rosannah Clark died on October 19, 1871; she was buried in the Brown-Hill Cemetery in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  Bradford married Polly Schenck in 1873.

In 1880, Bradford and Polly Clark are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one son living with them, Joseph.
