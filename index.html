WELCOME to PROOF-GENEALOGY.com

Proof-genealogy.com is a privately owned website dedicated to providing genealogical information that is accurate, in-depth, and verified.  Each page not only tells you the vital information about an ancestor, it also provides some form of proof, such as:

A photo of your ancestor
A newspaper obituary
A gravesite photo
Stories that help you get to know your ancestors
E-mail addresses of the people who submitted the information

Privacy Policy

This website has a policy of protecting the privacy of the living.   All of the information you will see on this website is about those who are deceased -- any information about their living descendants will be kept private.
