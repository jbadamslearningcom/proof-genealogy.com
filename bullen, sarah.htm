Marriage
Sarah Bullen married John Easton on July 20, 1841
in Holywell cum Needingworth, Huntingdonshire, England.


Sarah and John immigrated from England to America in 1856.
John died some time between 1856 and 1858.

Sarah married Christopher Stimpson on August 16, 1858
in Kankakee County, Illinois.  Christopher was also from England.

Children
Some family members have suggested that Sarah had
children; there is no known record of these children.
 
Notes
After their mother Elizabeth Kirby died, Sarah helped raise her younger brothers.

John and Sarah Eason appear on the 1851 census as living in Holywell, Cambridgeshire, England.  He is 35 years old and working as an agricultural laborer.  She is listed as being 28 years old.

In 1860, Christopher and Sarah Stimpson are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.
Their name appears as "Stephenson."  Sarah's brothers Robert and John are living with them.

In 1870, Christopher and Sarah Stimpson are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.

In 1880, Cris and Sarah Stimpson are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.

Sarah is buried next to her husband in Mound Grove Cemetery in Kankakee, Illinois.
 Their plot is adjacent to the family plot of Sarah's brother John Burling.
