Notes
  John Bullen was baptized on the 7 Dec 1794, in the Boxworth Parish Church. Parents are Edward and Sarah Bullen. in the Parish Records.

John Bullen appears on the 1841 census, living in Needingworth, Huntingdonshire, England.  He is 45 years old and working as a agricultural laborer.  He also has two sons living with him, James, age 15; and Samuel, age 11.

John Bullen appears on the 1851 census, living in Needingworth, Huntingdonshire, England.  He is 55 years old and working as a agricultural laborer.  He also has two sons living with him, Robert, age 18; and John, age 15.

John Bullen appears on the 1871 census as working as a farm laborer and living alone in Holywell cum Needingworth, Huntingdonshire, England.  At the time of the census, he is listed as being 77 years old.
