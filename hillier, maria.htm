Marriage

Mary M. Hillier married (?) Knox.  It is not known
when or where this marriage occurred.

Her second marriage was to Edward Mathews.  They married
on December 22, 1861; it is not known where they married.

Children (in birth order)
1
Maxwell
Mathews
1873-1891
 
Maria may have had other children besides Maxwell.  One possible child, Henry C.
Mathews, appears on the 1870 census.  Henry was born in 1856, before Maria
married Edward Mathews.  Henry may have been Maria's child by her first marriage,
or he may have been the product of a previous marriage by Edward Mathews.
Notes

In 1870, Edward and Maria Mathews are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County,
Illinois.  They have one child living with them, Henry C. Mathews, age 13, and born in Illinois.

After Maria's death, Edward married Sabra Mae Cegal and moved to
Minnesota and then South Dakota; they had four children together.
