Marriage

Clara Goodfellow married Charles Franklin Pierce
on April 12, 1879 in Kankakee, Illinois.
They had four children together; Charles died in 1898.

Clara's second marriage was to Volero Alberti Hathaway
on October 8, 1910 in Iroquois County, Illinois.
It was the second marriage for both of them.

Notes

In 1880, Charles and Clara Pierce are living in the household of Abiel  and
Mary Pierce, Charles's parents, in Adams Township, LaSalle County, Illinois.

In 1920, V.A. and Clara Hathaway are living in Martinton Township, Iroquois
County, Illinois.  They have one son living with them, Neal, who is believed to
be V.A. Hathaway's son from a previous marriage.
