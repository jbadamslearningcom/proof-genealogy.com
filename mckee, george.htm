Notes

In 1850, George and Ann McKee are living in Campton Township, Kane County, Illinois.  They have their eldest seven children living with them.

In 1860, George and Ann McKee are living in Ganeer Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them, John, James, Sarah, and William.
