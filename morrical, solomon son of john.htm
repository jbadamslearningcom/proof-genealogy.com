Notes
In 1840, Solomon and Fanny Morrical are living in Butler Township,
Darke County, Ohio.  They have no children living with them.

In 1850, Solomon and Fanny Morrical are living in Harrison Township,
Blackford County, Indiana.  They have five children listed living with them.

In 1860, Solomon and Fanny Morrical are living in Jackson Township,
Blackford County, Indiana.  They have nine children listed living with them.

In 1870, Solomon and Fanny Morrical are living in Harrison Township,
Blackford County, Indiana.  They have six children listed living with them.
Five-year-old Almaretta Morrical is listed as being their daughter.

In 1880, Fanny Morrical is widowed and living in Harrison Township,
Blackford County, Indiana.  She has one son, Robert, age 19, living
with her, as well as one granddaughter, Almaretta Morrical, age 15.
