Notes

In 1860, Guilford and Jane Brown are living in Wygandt Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have two children living with them, Charles and John D.  They also have 65-year-old Jacob Seper living with them; this may be Jane's father.

In 1870, Guilford and Jane Brown are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them, Charles, John, Anna, and Libby.

In 1880, Guilford Brown is living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  He has four children living with him, John, Alma, Libby, and Ellie.
