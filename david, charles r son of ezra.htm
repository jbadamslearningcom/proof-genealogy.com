Notes
In 1880, Charles and his wife Jane are living in the household of Ezra and Eliza David in Douglas Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  Arthur David is also living with them, as are Eliza's two sons, William and Sirus.

In 1920, Charles and Jennie David are living in Mapleton Township, Lane County, Oregon.  They have one child living with them, Ray.  Bruce and family are living next door.

In 1930, Charles and Jennie David are living in Mapleton Township, Lane County, Oregon.  They have one child living with them, Ray.

Charles died in 1951, Jennie died January 21, 1953 in Lane County.
