Notes

In 1860, Finley Morrical is five years old and living in the home of William and Lolita Plummer in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.

In 1870, Finley is living in the home of William and Lolita Plummer in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.

 In 1880, Finley and Jane Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have two children living with them.

 In 1900, Finley and Mary Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have four children living with them.

 In 1910, Finley and Sarah Morrical are living in Tippecanoe Township, Marshall County, Indiana.  They have no children living with them.

Finley's wife, Sarah Hissong Morrical, died on September 29, 1928.

Finley and Sarah Morrical and family are buried in the
 Parks Memorial Cemetery in Bourbon, Indiana
