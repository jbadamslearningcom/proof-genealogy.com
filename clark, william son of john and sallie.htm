Notes

In 1850, William and Sarah Clark are living in Union Township, Bartholomew County, Indiana.  They have six children living with them:  Elmira, Rebecca, Orine, Mahala, James, and Labitha.

In 1860, William and Sarah Clark are living in Wygandt Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have seven children living with them:  Orinn, Mahala, James, Latitia, Horace, Benjamin, and Eunice.

In 1870, William and Sarah Clark are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them:  James, Horace, and Benjamin.

In 1880, William and Sarah Clark are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one son living with them, Benjamin.

Sarah "Sallie" Clark died March 7, 1885.
