Notes
 

According to genealogist Wilman Davis, Eliza was known for assisting Dr. Wesley Mathieu of St. Anne in bringing many babies into this world.  All of Lovina's and most of Leroy's children came into being in this way.  Many know of the Franklins who resided at the first place west of the home farm (the Benjamin-Davis farm).  When Harold Franklin was a little boy, he had the misfortune of dumping a lye solution (used in making soap) all over his face and body.  His mother carried him over to Eliza's.  She had a new can of linseed oil which she poured all over Harold.  When the doctor arrived, he told her that she had done well -- probably saved him and his eyes in particular -- but next time she should use vinegar.
