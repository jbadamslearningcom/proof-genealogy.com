Notes
In 1870, William and Jemima David are living Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have five children living with them.  The household also shows that Peter Maybee, age 35, is living with them.  (This is believed to be Jemima's brother.)

In 1880, William and Jemima David are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  There are no children living with them; however, two others are in the household with them:  Peter Strigel, age 23, laborer; and Caroline Benjamin, age 13, servant.

This blurb appeared April 3, 1901, in the Watseka Republican.

This death notice appeared March 29, 1901 in the Chebanse Herald.
