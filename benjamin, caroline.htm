Marriage

Caroline Benjamin married William H. Hoffman
on July 30, 1882 in Iroquois County, Illinois.  They divorced in 1889.

William H. Hoffman married Ella McKimpson
on December 2, 1889 in Iroquois County, Illinois.

Caroline Benjamin married Patrick Langan (a.k.a. Peter R. Langdon)
on October 25, 1899 in Champaign County, Illinois.

Notes

In 1893, Maud Huffman is listed in the Urbana city directory,
living at 1413 Church.

In 1895-1896, Maud Hoffman is listed in the Champaign city directory,
living at 402 E. Columbia, as the widow of William.

In 1900, William and Nellie Hoffman are living in Chicago, Cook County, Illinois; they have one child living with them, Jennie.

In 1910, Patrick and Carrie Langan are living in Louisville, Jefferson County, Kentucky.

In 1910, William and Elly Hoffman are living in Sheldon Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.

Patrick R. Langan died February 5, 1913, in Jefferson County, Kentucky.

After Carrie's death, the Langan and Benjamin families
fought over the Langan estate in the courts.

Carrie's body is believed to have been  interred in the mausoleum built by her sister, Nell Clark.  After Nell's death, the mausoleum is supposed to have contained five bodies:  Carrie Benjamin Langan, Sylvia Wadley Smith, Arthur Clark, Gertrude Clark Berchier, and Nellie Clark.  In 1991, the deceased in the mausoleum were buried in unmarked graves, and today the mausoleum stands empty.

William Hoffman died January 24, 1917, in Sheldon Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.
Ella Hoffman died  May 29, 1922, in Sheldon Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.

William H. Hoffman's obituary does not specifically
mention Caroline Benjamin as his first wife.
