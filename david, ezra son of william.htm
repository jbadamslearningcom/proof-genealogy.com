Notes

In 1880, Ezra and Angeline David are living in Aroma Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them:  Caroline, Nellie, and Cora Bell.

In 1900, Ezra and Angeline David are living in Aroma Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have four children living with them:  Bert, Lillie, Effie, and Ira.

At the time of his death in 1909, Ezra's three oldest daughters had died.

Angeline David (Mrs. Louis Francoeur) died on October 26, 1938 in Kankakee County, Illinois.

The death notice below was printed in the Kankakee Daily Gazette on January 5, 1910.

This obituary was printed in the St. Anne Record, January 7, 1910.
