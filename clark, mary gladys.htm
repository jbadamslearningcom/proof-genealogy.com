Notes
In 1900, Gladys Clark is living with her parents and sisters in Concord Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.

In 1910, Samuel and Gladys Clarke are living in Concord Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have one child living with them, Vivian, age 3/12.

In 1920, Samuel and Gladys Clarke are living in North Township, Lake County, Indiana.  They have one child living with them, Vivian, age 9.  Also living with them are Gladys's parents, N. Major and S. May Clarke.

In 1930, Samuel and Gladys Clarke are living in North Township, Lake County, Indiana.  They have one child living with them, Gloria, age 8.  Also living with them is George Boswell, a roomer.

Samuel Clarke died October 13, 1954.

Gladys has an obituary printed in the Hammond Times.
