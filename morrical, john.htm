Notes
In 1810, John Morricle is living in Montgomery
County, Virginia with his wife and four children.

In 1820, John Morricle is living in Montgomery
County, Virginia with his wife and nine children.

In 1830, John Morricle is living in Montgomery
County, Virginia with his wife and twelve children.
It is not known if all the children belong to John and his wife.

In 1831, John sold his land in Virginia and moved west
with his wife and family, settling in Darke County, Ohio.

In 1860, John is living in the household of his son Andrew in Jay County, Indiana.

In late 1860 or early 1861, John went to live with the family
of his son Clark, in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.

On November 1, 1864, John Morrical died in the home of his son
Clark's family, in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.
He was buried in Evergreen Cemetery in Chebanse, Illinois.
