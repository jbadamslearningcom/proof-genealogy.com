Notes

Dora's mother Sarah died when Dora was only five years old.  Dora's father, Elias, gave up his daughters to let them live with their aunts and uncles.

In 1880, Dora was living with Alfred and Mary Pace in Wells County, Indiana.  Mary Adams Pace was a sister to Dora's mother, Sarah.
