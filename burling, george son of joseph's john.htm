Notes

COLFAX — George Burling 81, Rt. 1, Wheeler, died Friday at Luther Hospital. He was born Sept. 5, 1889, in Indiana, and came to this area at an early age. In 1913 he married Alta Card, who died in 1919. He married Cora Jones in 1921 and they farmed in Colfax until 1940, when they moved to the Town of Otter Creek, where they farmed until retirement. Besides his wife, he is survived by one son, Donald, Hillsdale; three daughters, Mrs Clarance Snyder, Wheeler, Mrs. Richard Smith, Barron, Mrs. Orrin Smith, Sandia Park, New Mexico; a brother William, Chicago; 16 grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be at 2 p.m. Tuesday at the Colfax United Methodist Church with Rev. Burton Rolling officiating. Burial will be in the Hill Grove Cemetery. Friends may call at the Goodrich Funeral Home in Colfax Monday afternoon and evening, Tuesday morning and from 12:30 p.m. until time of services at the church. The Eau Claire Leader June 1971
