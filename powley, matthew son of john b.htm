Below:  The obituaries and gravestone for Matthew Powley's wife, Annie.

  

Annie shares a gravestone with two of her children, Susie and
Hurbert.  Both children died as infants.  Hurbert was born in
January 1878 and died after only 19 days.  Annie died three weeks later.
