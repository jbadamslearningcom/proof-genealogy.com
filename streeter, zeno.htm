Marriage

Zeno Streeter married Mary Maria Everett
on September 21, 1845, in McHenry County, Illinois.
Mary died in 1868.

After Mary died, he married Maria Grant Streeter on December 31, 1869 in Kankakee County, Illinois.  Maria was the widow of Zeno's brother Zenas.  Maria died in 1874.

After Maria died, he married Catherine (?) Waters, on April 16, 1876, in Kankakee County, Illinois.

Notes

In 1850, Zeno and Mary Mariah Streeter are living in Richmond Township, McHenry County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Adelbert, Francis, and George.  They are living next door to the David Everett family.

In 1860, Zeno and Mary Streeter are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Adelbert, Francis, George, Orin, and Charles.  They are living next door to the David Everett family.

In 1870, Zenas and Moriah Streeter are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have seven children living with them, Francis, George, Orrin, Charles, Alma, Louisa, and William.

In 1880, Zeno and Catharine Streeter are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have two children living with them, Orrin and Charles, and one niece, Louisa.
