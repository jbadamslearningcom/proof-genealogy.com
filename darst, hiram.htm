Marriage

Hiram Darst married married Mary Jones on September 5, 1850.
They had three children together; Mary died in 1855.

Hiram Darst married Amanda Lawhead on November 5, 1857.
Amanda was born August 29, 1837, in Ohio.  They had three
children together; Amanda died on February 24, 1868.

Hiram Darst married Abigail Warren in the late 1860s.
Abigail was born in about 1849 in New York.  They
are not known to have had any children together.
