Notes
In 1900, Faye Clark is living with her parents and sisters in Concord Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.

In 1910, Major and May Clark are living in Concord Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have two children living with them, Fay and Frances.  They also have one grandchild living with them, Nita Smith.  (Nita is Faye's daughter.)  The census indicates that Faye is a widow.

In 1930, Ira and Faye Clarke are living in Concord Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have two children living with them, Betty Jane and Billy Eugene.

Ira Clarke died on May 2, 1968 in Iroquois County.
