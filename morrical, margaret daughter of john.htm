Notes
In 1850, Isaac and Margaret Harter are living in Butler Township,
Darke County, Ohio.  They have eight children listed living with them.

Isaac Harter died in October 1850.  According to the Harter family history written by Byron Harter, "Following Isaac's untimely death, Michael Zeeck, a prominent citizen of New Madison, Ohio, was named the guardian of Eli, Mary, Catherine, and John.  There is no mention of William, Diana, or Francis in the final settlement of Isaac's estate."

In 1860, Margaret Harter is a widow living in Butler Township, Darke County, Ohio.  She has six children living with her.

In 1870, Margaret Harter is living in Butler Township, Darke County, Ohio.  She has three adult children living with her.

 

Harter Cemetery is located near the intersection of Harter Road and State Road 121.
