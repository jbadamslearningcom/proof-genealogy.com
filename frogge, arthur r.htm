Notes

In 1860, Arthur and Susannah Frogge are living in Beaver Township,
Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have six children living with them. 

In 1870, Arthur and Susannah Frogge are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois
County, Illinois.  They have their four youngest children living with them.

In 1880, Arthur and Susannah Frogge are living in Papineau Township, Iroquois County,
Illinois.  Their son Benton is living with them, as is their grandson, James Hale.

Both Arthur and Susannah's graves were recorded in 1976.  In 2004,
neither of their graves can be located in the Beaver Creek Cemetery.
