Notes
William as a young man.

In 1920, William and family were living in Woodbury County, Iowa.

In 1930, William and family were living in Cabery, Illinois.

In 1936, William moved his family to Los Angeles, California.
There he worked as a watchman in an aircraft factory.

These death notices were printed in the Santa Monica Evening Standard.
 William died October 2, 1945.  Helma died November 4, 1953.

William and Helma Morrical are buried in Woodlawn Cemetery in
Santa Monica, California.  They are not buried next to each other.
