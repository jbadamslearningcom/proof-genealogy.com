Notes

In 1860, William H. and Harriet Griffin are living in Wygandt Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them, Henry, George, and Chester.  Also living with them is William's mother, Sarah.

In 1870, William H. and Harriet Griffin are living in Martinton Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  They have five children living with them, George, and Chester B., Amanda, Maria, and John.  Also living with them is William's mother, Sarah.

In 1880, Harriet Griffin is living in Martinton Township, Iroquois County, Illinois.  She has three children living with her George, Bradford, and Alvin.
