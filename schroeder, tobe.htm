Notes

Standing in back:  Annie, Marie, Minnie, Tracy;
sitting in front:  Christina, Frauke, Tobe, Cornelius, and Henry.

Tobe and Frauke Schroeder departed from Bremen, Germany, and came to America with their children aboard the ship Mosel in 1874.  They arrived in New York on May 9, 1874.
