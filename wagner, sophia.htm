Marriage
Sophia Wagner married Adoph Hummel in Germany.
Together they had one son, Henry.  Adolph died in the mid 1860s.

Sophia then married Karl Kopke in 1866.  When they came to
America in 1872, the spelling of their name changed to "Copke"
 and her husband changed his name to Charles Copke.

After her husband Charles died in 1906, she married John Jacob Walters
on September 26, 1907 in Kankakee, Illinois.  J.J. was also from Germany.

Notes
In 1880, Charles and Sophia Copke are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have six children living with them:  Augusta, Irena, Lenia, May, Minne, and Charles.  Their name is listed as "Cup."

In 1900, Charles and Sophia Copke are living in Otto Township, Kankakee County, Illinois.  They have three children living with them:  Charles, Ernest, and Mabel.

In 1910, John Jacob Walters and his wife Sophia are living in Kankakee, Illinois.

Sophia's parents' names were obtained from her 1907 marriage license.
